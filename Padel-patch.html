Overview

Padel Patch is a dynamic platform designed to bring together sports enthusiasts, clubs, and facilities through clean design and intuitive user flow. The goal was to enhance booking and community engagement within the rapidly growing sport of padel.

What is the Problem?

Despite the rising popularity of padel, many clubs and players still face difficulties in booking courts, managing schedules, and building an active community. The lack of a centralized, user-friendly platform hinders smooth experiences for both players and facility owners.

Project Goal

The goal of this project is to design a clean, intuitive, and responsive platform that simplifies court bookings, enhances communication between players and clubs, and promotes the sport through a seamless digital experience.
